What is Music

Music is a reverberation of many tunes and voices at the same time.It has the power of evoking emotions in a human being.It unites and brings us together as a human race.Depending on the genre you choose you'd sure love it in any way

Hip-Hop

This is a type of Music that's characterised by heavy beats and elements of black culture.It tends to highlight on many of the societal issues that affect the society.It is a widly popular genre among the youth.Although many folks associate it with violence and an agent of vices in the society it can be used as an element of change in the society where the youth can use it as a channel of them airing out their issues

Pop

A genre which has elements of having contemporary beats and does not follow a strict musical flow and rules.Artistes are left to experiment with different styles and beats.If one can try to check between this and Hip-Hop Pop is more popular globally

Gospel

This is a music genre which is influenced by the christian teachings and beliefs.Truly it is an uplifting genre which you can listen to anytime.It features many great acts like Kirk Franklin,the mary mary sisters and other singers
